What's a planted tank?

A planted tank is a tank that has multiple layers of substrate and features live plants growing in this media. The lowest layer is typically dirt, while the top layer is composed of a granular material that weighs the dirt down. Plants thrive off of the dirt, while the top layer provides some more aquarium feel. This creates a more realistic underwater environment that changes and grows with the conditions of the tank. This also comes with drawbacks, such as requiring delicate care and water maintenance.
